About Me

My name is Andrew Winters; I am 29 years old at the time of writing, and I am currently attending the 3-week Develop Course at Code Nation in Chester.

I have some previous experience of programming, having developed an interest in the subject after finding a copy of the book, The C Programming Language, in a charity shop in 2008 (I still have it). In time I progressed to looking into various other programming topics, particularly web development, and I do still have some of the - perhaps slightly outdated - work from that time period. However, due to work commitments, I let this interest fall somewhat into the background.

One thing that did endure from this time period is my use of the Linux operating system at home, and I like to think that this has kept my IT skills somewhat more up-to-date than they might otherwise be, since using this OS day-to-day does occasionally require some in-depth problem solving.

Recently, whilst working at Chester Jobcentre near the end of a fixed-term contract, I came to learn of Code Nation through their admissions team, who were visiting the site to talk to staff and customers about the courses they offer. I took a look at their website and concluded that taking this course would be an excellent step in the right direction. I would like, at this point in my life, to start a career, ideally doing something that I have an interest in; I enjoy coding and problem solving generally, and I relish the idea of developing usable products whilst continuously learning and updating my skills within the industry.
